Tim Barnett

A Little Rock native, husband, and father of two with over 10 years of hands-on experience in the lighting and landscaping industry. Tim started Rock City Lighting with one goal: to give Central Arkansas homeowners access to the kind of exterior lighting that used to be reserved for high-end custom builds.

Every project is handled personally — no franchise, no national chain. Just real attention to your home and your goals.

Dedicated to one thing: a satisfied customer, every single time.

Our Services

A complete menu of residential exterior lighting — designed, installed, and maintained by Rock City Lighting.

01

Architectural Uplighting

Highlight trees, facades, columns, stone, and rooflines. Make your home's best architectural features glow with precision after dark.

02

Pathway Lighting

Make walkways, steps, drives, and outdoor paths easier and safer to navigate. Beautiful by design, practical by nature.

03

Porch & Entry Lighting

Light the path to your front door. Step lighting, pillar uplighting, and entry-area fixtures that make a polished first impression from the street.

04

Landscape Lighting

Add depth, warmth, and structure to yards, gardens, and outdoor living spaces. Every corner of your property can look its best.

05

Outdoor Living & Entertaining

Transform patios, decks, pool surrounds, and outdoor entertaining areas into spaces you actually want to use after dark.

06

Maintenance & Bulb Plans

Keep your system clean, bright, and working properly. We handle the upkeep so your lighting always looks the way it should.
